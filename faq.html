Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nexus, pricing, security, and getting started.

What is Nexus and who is it for?+

Nexus is a comprehensive SaaS platform that helps teams of any size automate workflows, integrate tools, and gain insights from their data. We serve startups, mid-market companies, and enterprises. Whether you're in marketing, operations, HR, finance, or customer success, Nexus has solutions for you.

How secure is my data?+

Your data security is our top priority. We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. All data is backed up multiple times daily across geographically distributed data centers. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant. Every change is logged in our immutable audit logs.

How long does implementation take?+

Most teams are up and running in 3-5 days. Our onboarding specialists guide you through setup, your first workflows, and team training. For enterprise customers, we offer dedicated implementation support. The average customer sees measurable productivity gains within the first week.

Can I integrate Nexus with my existing tools?+

Yes! Nexus integrates with 500+ popular applications including Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Google Workspace, Zapier, and more. We provide pre-built connectors, a REST API, and webhooks for custom integrations. If your tool doesn't have a connector, our API makes custom integration straightforward.

What kind of support do you offer?+

All customers get 24/7 email and chat support. Professional plan customers get priority support with 1-hour response times. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager, phone support, and live training sessions. We also have comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and community forums.

Is there a free trial?+

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. You can build workflows, set up integrations, and explore analytics without any limitations. If you need more time to evaluate, contact our sales team for an extended evaluation period.
